Ayu System Modules

Fully Loaded Clinical Ecosystem

Every single screen is built with modern workflows to help your clinic achieve zero administrative overhead, optimized patient care, and flawless inventory control.

Patient Onboarding & EMR

Register new patients inside a streamlined EMR wizard. Maintain detailed profiles, contact numbers, and view complete history logs including past vital records, symptom cards, and billing summaries.

Token Queue & Appointments

Seamless receptionist check-in flow with automatically computed token queue numbers. Live doctor dashboard updates in real-time, matching queue tokens with active consulting rooms.

Real-Time Billing Cart

Generate fully itemized invoices. Directly edit drug unit prices and quantities inside the billing table with instantaneous calculations, split payments (cash/card), and inventory checks.

Stock & Expiry Batches

Maintain detailed inventory logs with custom stock reorder thresholds. Tracks separate batches with specific expiry dates and cost prices. Automatically deducts quantities on invoice creation.

Follow-Up Appointments Tracker

Add "Next Clinic Date" directly inside doctor consultations. Tracks overdue, due today, and upcoming follow-ups in a calendar schedule view, with a one-click consult routing button.

WhatsApp Automation

Keep patients updated. Automated WhatsApp templates dispatch queue tokens on register, check-in alerts, receipt invoice links, and scheduled follow-up clinic date reminders.

Smart Diagnosis Drug Presets

Allows pre-configuring set drug templates for matching diagnoses. Selecting the diagnosis in billing instantly populates the invoice cart, eliminating manual item typing.

Role-Based Permissions (RBAC)

Configure access for Admins, Doctors, Receptionists, and Cashiers. Administrators can dynamically specify allowed sidebar tabs for each user to maintain data security.

Analytics & Revenue Dashboard

Live stats counters displaying total patient counts, visits, collections, outstanding bills, and cost of goods sold (COGS) to calculate net margins dynamically.
